--- title: 'Amazon Wishlist Plugin' id: '34' author: 'Chris Benard' layout: post.html.twig category: Personal time: '22:50:21' tags: [ personal ] --- I've added my Amazon Wishlist to my blog. It lets people see what I want and link to it to buy it from the Amazon site. This was made possible because of a great plugin that Ryan Prins made. After downloading it, I noticed a few issues that made the XHTML not validate and a small functionality issue. I fixed them on my side and created a simple patch to fix the issues. After I sent it to Ryan via an email, he quickly updated it and thanked me on his site. I recommend you check out his plugin if you use Wordpress and the Amazon wishlist functionaity.
